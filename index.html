LearnVCS

LearnVCS

Bloxeo

React.js, SCSS

Bloxeo is a collaborative brainstorming web application. Team members work together to generate, combine, and vote on ideas in order to narrow the focus of or create a goal for a project.

Bloxeo Design

Break It

Swift (iOS)

Break It is a casual mobile game for iOS devices. The player rotates a color wheel in the center of the screen while colored balls spawn and move towards the wheel. To progress to more difficult levels, the player must rotate the color wheel so that balls hit and destroy corresponding colors on the wheel.

PicMe

Node.js, MongoDB, Jade, MVC

PicMe is a multiuser web application where users can post images to share them with other users. Register and login to start sharing and see the pictures that your friends have shared.

PicMe

Map My Product

HTML5, JavaScript, jQuery, JSON

Map My Product is an HTML5 Web App Mashup. It is a product locator using the Best Buy Product, Best Buy Stores, and Google Maps APIs. Search for a product and find Best Buy stores within your chosen radius that have the product in stock.
